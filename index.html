About

I am a researcher and engineer with a background in artificial intelligence, computer vision, deep learning, and sensing systems. My work focuses on developing practical AI-based perception methods that connect physical sensing modalities with robust machine perception.

I am currently pursuing an industrial PhD with imec and Vrije Universiteit Brussel (VUB). I am affiliated with the SENSE group at imec and the ETRO group at VUB, working on radar perception and machine learning for human sensing.

I received my M.S. in Information and Communication Engineering from Chosun University, South Korea, where I worked on deep learning-based image/video denoising, noisy dataset generation, skin lesion classification, and segmentation. I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from North South University, Bangladesh.

Selected Projects

Radar-Based Human Pose Estimation

Current doctoral research direction focused on estimating human pose from radar measurements under challenging sensing conditions where camera-based systems may be limited.

FMCW radar · radar perception · pose estimation · deep learning

Smart In-Cabin Monitoring System

Worked on AI-based in-cabin monitoring systems for autonomous vehicles, including driver monitoring and occupancy monitoring using facial landmarks, gaze estimation, segmentation, classification, and detection.

DMS · OMS · computer vision · edge AI

Semantic Segmentation and Object Detection on Edge Devices

Developed and deployed semantic segmentation and object detection models for autonomous vehicle applications on Texas Instruments TDA4VM edge devices.

segmentation · object detection · TDA4VM · deployment

Calibration and Pseudo-LiDAR from Monocular Camera

Developed a pseudo-LiDAR solution using monocular camera input, involving camera-LiDAR calibration, depth estimation, point cloud generation, and comparative evaluation against LiDAR data.

pseudo-LiDAR · depth estimation · point cloud · calibration

Image and Video Denoising / Noise Generation

Generated paired noisy-clean image and video datasets using GANs, attention mechanisms, and Vision Transformers to address dataset scarcity for denoising models.

GANs · Vision Transformers · denoising · restoration

Skin Lesion Classification and Segmentation

Improved skin lesion classification and segmentation using deep learning architectures for medical image analysis.

medical imaging · segmentation · classification · U-Net
